PROJECT CONCEPT

What We Want to Build

The Problem

Visitors around Pingjiang Road often struggle to decide where to go, what nearby places are worth visiting, and how to plan a route that fits their time, interests, and travel pace.

Our Solution

We propose a smart guide experience that combines place-based recommendations, short cultural introductions, and AI-assisted support to make local exploration easier and more engaging.

Target Users

Our project focuses on first-time visitors, young travelers, and time-limited tourists who want a smoother way to explore Suzhou's historic district.
