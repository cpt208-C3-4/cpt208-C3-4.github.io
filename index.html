Project Concept / THE VISION

Core Challenges

Fitness enthusiasts often struggle to scientifically track their training volume. Traditional paper logs are easy to lose, and generic apps are often too cluttered, breaking the flow of a workout.

Our Solution

We are building a minimalist, high-efficiency logging app. Featuring rapid input widgets and automated volume heatmaps, we make your physical progress visually undeniable.

Target Audience

Designed for consistent gym-goers looking to break plateaus and self-disciplined individuals who need systematic management of their athletic data.

User Personas / CASE STUDIES

Alex Chen

Office Junior / Gym Novice

AGE: 24

FREQ: 2 days/week

Primary Goal

Improve posture caused by desk work, achieve weight loss, and build a consistent fitness habit.

Pain Points

Often feels intimidated by complex equipment and doesn't know where to start. Because he lacks a logging habit, he spends half his workout trying to recall what weight he used last time, leading to a disjointed experience. Without seeing tangible proof of progress, he finds it hard to stay motivated during the slow early stages.

Sarah Lin

Intermediate / Gym Regular

AGE: 29

FREQ: 4-5 days/week

Primary Goal

Break through strength plateaus and refine body composition through disciplined progressive overload.

Pain Points

Currently stuck at a strength plateau and needs data-driven insights to adjust. Her manual phone notes are scattered and make it impossible to track Total Training Volume over monthly cycles. She feels the lack of a "quick-look" analytics tool prevents her from fine-tuning her split efficiently.
